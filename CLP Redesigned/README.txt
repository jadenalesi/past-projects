##CLP Redesigned##

This represents my contributions to my Senior Project at Saint Vincent. I worked with a group of four other students to effectively redesinging how the college collected attendance data
from those sessions.

For my part, I've always been a guy who's best under the hood, so I was in charge of making sure we had a solid backend. This represents the majority of my work on this project. 

The biggest problems I had with this project came from our production database not matching the database the frontend team was working with. This meant that a lot of my efforts,
after API call creation, came in actually retouching the frontend so it could handle the data it was being sent. A fun part of the development process is trying to get things to fit together
and realizing the left hand probably should've told the right hand what it was doing just a little sooner.

This was also a fun excercise in working with an "employer". The college had a certain way they wanted us to do things, and we had to adhere to that pretty strictly. Testing revealed we were never
going to make everyone happy, so the group focused more on trying to deliver the product the people using it the most wanted. Despite this, we still had constraints to work with, and it was interesting
seeing how we could strecth those limits without breaking them.
