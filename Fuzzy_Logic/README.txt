This is a Fuzzy Logic program I helped to create as part of an Introduction to Artificial Intelligence project.

This program is designed to take the inputs of several different categories from a customer satisfaction survey and determine their overall satisfaction, their likley loyalty, and the individual retention risk of the customer using fuzzy logic. 

For this project, I was responsible for the creation of much of the rules base as well as consulting on other parts of the program to determine the optimal path for outupts. One challenge of this project was deciding exactly how to weigh individual results and categories.
